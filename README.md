# esp32Generic
A generic firmware for esp32 with easy setup, OTA updates, mqtt support and more.

PIN assignments.
* LED: 2
* BUTTON: 13
* ACTORS
** PUMP: 5
** VALVE_OPEN: 19
** VALVE_CLOSE: 18
* SENSORS
** FLOW_TEMP: 32
** RETURN_TEMP: 35
** OUTSIDE_TEMP: 34
* TFT
** CS: 15
** DC: 0
** RST: 2
** MOSI: 8
** MISO: 7 
** SCLK: 6
